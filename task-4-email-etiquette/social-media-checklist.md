# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post
- Use privacy settings wisely
- Share accurate and verified information
- Be respectful in comments and messages
- Maintain a positive digital image

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or rumors
- Avoid cyberbullying or negative comments
- Do not use unprofessional language publicly

# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔒 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via email, SMS, or social media.
- Always verify the sender before sharing any personal information.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
- Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security updates.

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone.
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” in UPI apps.
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment.

## 📢 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Helpline: 1930 (for immediate financial fraud assistance)

## ⚠️ Reminder
Stay alert and think before you click or share. Awareness is your first line of defense against cybercrime.
